About Dr. Care O'Lot & Puppy Care O'Lot

Welcome to the world of Dr. Care O'Lot and her adorable sidekick Puppy Care O'Lot! We're here to teach kids fun, easy-to-understand health tips. Through our fun videos, we make learning about health exciting! From brushing teeth to staying active, we cover everything kids need to know to stay healthy and happy.

Created by Patrick Daniel Duffy, our mission is to help kids and parents learn about good health habits in a fun and interactive way. Join us and learn how to stay healthy while having fun!
